Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard
This project presents a dynamic and interactive Excel dashboard designed to provide a 360-degree view of an e-commerce businessâ€™s sales performance across different dimensions.

Objective
To transform raw  data into meaningful insights through data visualization, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions and monitor (KPIs) effectively.

Features
Main Dashboard: A centralized view of all metrics including total sales, profit, and order volumes with filters for dynamic insights.

Track sales and profit trends over time for performance evaluation.

Decompose profit/loss contribution by category or region to identify key impact areas.

Visual breakdown of revenue and profitability across product categories or customer segments.

Geographical distribution of sales across the United States, helping to identify strong and weak regions.

Comparative analysis of sales and profit by product category, region, or year.

Clear, concise performance indicators (e.g., Total Sales, YOY Growth, Top Region, Top Product).

Year-over-Year (YOY) Comparison: Analyzes growth trends and helps identify areas of improvement or strategic focus.

Outcome
The dashboard empowers business analysts and decision-makers to:

Track performance trends

Identify top-performing products and regions

Evaluate customer segment profitability

Make strategic adjustments to marketing, or sales operations
